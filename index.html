About me

Hey, I’m Bowen. I'm a 3rd year at the University of Virginia studying CS and Data Science. I’m passionate about coding and software development, and hoping to learn more about ML and AI.

My hobbies include golfing, basketball, lifting weights, cooking, ocean kayaking, and video games. I’m a member of the club golf team at UVA. During the past few summers, I’ve enjoyed working as a guide for kayak tours around coastal Massachusetts. In the coming years, I plan to explore new places every chance I get and spend as much time as possible adventuring outdoors.

I’m beginning my journey into the professional world, starting with building cool software projects and learning as much as I can. I plan to apply my knowledge of coding to working with AI models and data. Lately, I’ve been building this portfolio as a place to showcase my future projects and experiences in the world of computing. Look out for new projects being added soon!
